Chicken Gumbo

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken thighs, boneless and skinless
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup sliced okra
  • 2 tsp Cajun seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked rice, for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat oil over medium heat. Whisk in flour and cook, stirring constantly, until the roux is a deep brown color.
  2. Add onion, bell pepper, celery, and garlic. Cook until vegetables are soft.
  3. Add chicken and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in chicken broth, tomatoes, okra, and Cajun seasoning. Bring to a simmer.
  5. Cover and cook for 30-40 minutes, until chicken is cooked through.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot over rice.
